Our Services

Offering comprehensive MEP engineering solutions, we ensure innovative, reliable, and sustainable designs for all your construction projects.

HVAC Engineering

As an authorized dealer for Bluestar and Hitachi, GKC Enginners Pvt ltd specializes in commercial air conditioning systems, including VRV, cassette, and tower ACs. We also offer basement ventilation, smoke management, ducting, lift well pressurization, and fresh air systems to ensure optimal performance and efficiency.

Electrical Engineering

We offer comprehensive electrical infrastructure services, including lighting design, power distribution, switchgear design, voice and TV networking, CCTV, earthing and lightning protection, campus lighting, substation equipment, DG sets, emergency power, IT networking, and solar plant
installation.

Public Health Engineering

We specialize in comprehensive water supply and management systems, covering soil and waste disposal, rainwater disposal, pumps and piping, water and sewage treatment plants, recycled water supply, stormwater drainage, rainwater harvesting, groundwater recharging, and solar hot water generation.
